# Metric Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the key business metrics used throughout the project.

The metrics are organized according to the e-commerce customer journey:

**Acquisition → Conversion → Revenue → Refund → Retention**

---

# Acquisition Metrics

| KPI                 | Definition                                     | Formula                             | Business Use                    |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Sessions            | Total number of website visits                 | Total website sessions              | Measure website traffic volume  |
| Users               | Total number of unique visitors                | Total unique users                  | Measure audience size           |
| Repeat Sessions     | Sessions generated by returning visitors       | Total sessions from returning users | Evaluate customer loyalty       |
| Repeat Session Rate | Percentage of sessions from returning visitors | Repeat Sessions ÷ Sessions          | Measure retention effectiveness |

---

# Conversion Metrics

| KPI                       | Definition                                     | Formula                       | Business Use                               |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Orders                    | Total completed purchases                      | Total completed orders        | Measure purchasing activity                |
| Conversion Rate           | Percentage of sessions resulting in a purchase | Orders ÷ Sessions             | Evaluate funnel effectiveness              |
| Conversion Rate by Source | Conversion performance of each traffic source  | Orders ÷ Sessions (by source) | Identify high-quality acquisition channels |
| Conversion Rate by Device | Conversion performance of each device type     | Orders ÷ Sessions (by device) | Evaluate user experience across devices    |

---

# Revenue Metrics

| KPI                       | Definition                                      | Formula                  | Business Use                     |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Revenue                   | Total sales generated from completed orders     | Sum of all order revenue | Measure business growth          |
| COGS                      | Total cost of goods sold                        | Sum of product costs     | Measure operating costs          |
| Gross Profit              | Revenue remaining after deducting product costs | Revenue − COGS           | Measure profitability            |
| Profit Margin             | Percentage of revenue retained as profit        | Gross Profit ÷ Revenue   | Evaluate financial performance   |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Average revenue generated per order             | Revenue ÷ Orders         | Assess transaction quality       |
| Revenue by Device         | Revenue contribution by device type             | Total revenue by device  | Identify revenue-driving devices |

---

# Refund Metrics

| KPI            | Definition                                  | Formula                     | Business Use                                      |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Refunded Items | Total number of refunded products           | Total refunded items        | Measure refund volume                             |
| Refund Amount  | Total value refunded to customers           | Sum of refund transactions  | Quantify refund impact                            |
| Refund Rate    | Percentage of sold items that were refunded | Refunded Items ÷ Sold Items | Monitor product quality and customer satisfaction |
| Net Revenue    | Revenue remaining after refunds             | Revenue − Refund Amount     | Measure actual business contribution              |

---

# Funnel Metrics

| KPI                   | Definition                                     | Formula                                        | Business Use                    |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Homepage Sessions     | Sessions that visited the homepage             | Total homepage sessions                        | Measure funnel entry traffic    |
| Product Page Sessions | Sessions that viewed product pages             | Total product page sessions                    | Measure product interest        |
| Cart Sessions         | Sessions that reached the cart page            | Total cart sessions                            | Measure purchase intent         |
| Checkout Sessions     | Sessions that reached checkout                 | Total checkout sessions                        | Measure purchase readiness      |
| Purchase Sessions     | Sessions that completed a purchase             | Total purchasing sessions                      | Measure funnel completion       |
| Funnel Drop-off Rate  | Percentage of users lost between funnel stages | (Previous Step − Current Step) ÷ Previous Step | Identify conversion bottlenecks |

---

# Attribution Metrics

| KPI                     | Definition                                                    | Formula                  | Business Use                          |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| First-Touch Conversions | Conversions attributed to the user's first acquisition source | Attribution model result | Understand acquisition effectiveness  |
| Last-Touch Conversions  | Conversions attributed to the final source before purchase    | Attribution model result | Measure closing-channel effectiveness |

---

# Retention Metrics

| KPI                           | Definition                                                  | Formula                         | Business Use                     |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Returning Users               | Users who visit the website more than once                  | Total returning users           | Measure customer loyalty         |
| Retention Rate                | Percentage of users who return after their first visit      | Returning Users ÷ Total Users   | Evaluate retention performance   |
| Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) | Average revenue generated by a customer over their lifetime | Total Revenue ÷ Total Customers | Measure long-term customer value |

---

# Executive Dashboard KPIs

The final dashboard will focus on the following core metrics:

| Category    | KPI                                             |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Acquisition | Sessions, Users, Repeat Session Rate            |
| Conversion  | Orders, Conversion Rate                         |
| Revenue     | Revenue, Gross Profit, AOV                      |
| Refund      | Refund Rate, Net Revenue                        |
| Retention   | Retention Rate, LTV                             |
| Attribution | First-Touch Attribution, Last-Touch Attribution |

---

# Metric Ownership

| Stakeholder        | Primary Metrics                                 |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Marketing          | Sessions, Conversion Rate, Attribution Metrics  |

| Finance            | Revenue, Gross Profit, Refund Rate, Net Revenue |
| Growth & Retention | Repeat Session Rate, Retention Rate, LTV        |
