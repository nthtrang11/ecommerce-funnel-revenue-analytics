# Stakeholder Requirements

## Overview

Different stakeholders evaluate business performance from different perspectives.

To ensure that analytical outputs support decision-making, stakeholder objectives must be translated into measurable analytical requirements.

This document identifies the primary stakeholders, their business goals, key concerns, and the analytical outputs required to support decision-making.

---

# Stakeholder Landscape

The project involves five major stakeholder groups:

| Stakeholder                   | Primary Focus                             |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) | Acquisition and campaign performance      |
| Product Manager               | Conversion funnel optimization            |
| Finance Team                  | Revenue quality and profitability         |
| Retention Team                | Customer retention and lifetime value     |
| Analytics Team                | Attribution and customer journey analysis |

---

# Marketing Stakeholder

## Business Objective

Improve marketing efficiency and maximize return on marketing investment.

## Business Challenge

The company acquires customers through multiple acquisition channels and campaigns.

As marketing spend increases, leadership requires greater visibility into which channels generate valuable customers and sustainable revenue growth.

Without clear measurement, budget allocation decisions may rely on assumptions rather than evidence.

## Analytical Requirements

The marketing team requires visibility into:

- Traffic acquisition performance
- Campaign effectiveness
- Conversion performance across channels
- Revenue contribution by acquisition source
- Customer quality across channels
- Marketing attribution performance

## Expected Business Impact

- Better marketing budget allocation
- Higher return on advertising spend
- Improved customer acquisition efficiency

---

# Product Stakeholder

## Business Objective

Improve website conversion performance and reduce customer drop-off.

## Business Challenge

A large number of visitors reach the website but only a small proportion complete a purchase.

The organization needs to understand where friction occurs within the customer journey and which stages of the funnel create the greatest barriers to conversion.

## Analytical Requirements

The product team requires visibility into:

- User navigation behavior
- Funnel progression
- Funnel abandonment patterns
- Device-specific user behavior
- Page engagement and session flow

## Expected Business Impact

- Higher conversion rates
- Improved user experience
- Reduced checkout abandonment

---

# Finance Stakeholder

## Business Objective

Protect profitability and improve financial visibility.

## Business Challenge

Revenue growth alone is insufficient to evaluate business performance.

Refunds, product costs, and customer purchasing behavior can significantly impact profitability.

Finance leaders require a clear understanding of the relationship between revenue generation and profit retention.

## Analytical Requirements

The finance team requires visibility into:

- Revenue performance
- Cost structure
- Gross margin
- Refund exposure
- Product profitability
- Net revenue contribution

## Expected Business Impact

- Improved profitability monitoring
- Reduced revenue leakage
- Better financial decision-making

---

# Retention Stakeholder

## Business Objective

Increase customer retention and long-term customer value.

## Business Challenge

Customer acquisition is expensive.

Sustainable growth depends on retaining existing customers and encouraging repeat purchases.

The company currently lacks a structured view of customer retention behavior and long-term customer value.

## Analytical Requirements

The retention team requires visibility into:

- Repeat visitor behavior
- Repeat purchase behavior
- Customer retention trends
- Cohort performance
- Customer lifetime value

## Expected Business Impact

- Higher customer retention
- Increased repeat purchases
- Improved long-term revenue growth

---

# Analytics Stakeholder

## Business Objective

Develop a complete understanding of customer acquisition and conversion behavior.

## Business Challenge

Customers often interact with multiple marketing channels before making a purchase.

Determining which touchpoints influence purchasing decisions remains a complex challenge.

The analytics team requires a structured framework for understanding customer journeys and attribution performance.

## Analytical Requirements

The analytics team requires visibility into:

- Customer journey behavior
- Attribution models
- Acquisition effectiveness
- Funnel performance
- Customer value generation

## Expected Business Impact

- Better attribution methodology
- Improved channel evaluation
- Stronger analytical decision support
