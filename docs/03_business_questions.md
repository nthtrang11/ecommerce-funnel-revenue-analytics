# Business Questions

## Purpose

This document translates stakeholder requirements into analytical questions that can be answered using data.

The objective is to bridge business needs and analytical execution by transforming stakeholder concerns into measurable business and data questions.

# Executive-Level Questions

The project ultimately aims to answer the following strategic questions:

1. Which acquisition channels drive the most valuable traffic?

2. Which marketing campaigns generate the greatest business impact?

3. Where are the largest conversion bottlenecks in the customer journey?

4. How does user behavior differ across device types?

5. What are the primary drivers of revenue growth?

6. How much revenue is being lost through refunds?

7. Which products create the greatest quality and profitability risks?

8. Which campaigns and channels generate the most loyal customers?

9. Which customer groups create the highest lifetime value?

10. How should marketing, product, finance, and growth teams prioritize future actions based on data-driven insights?
